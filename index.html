About

A Bridge Between Ecosystems

Born in the Dominican Republic and raised between Nicaragua and the United States, I've developed a unique multicultural perspective that drives my approach to venture capital. My journey spans from being a COO at 21, scaling a startup to 500+ employees and $42M raised, to becoming a Senior Associate at VU Venture Partners.

I specialize in early-stage investments across fintech, AI, healthcare, and crypto, with a particular focus on bridging North American and Latin American markets. My investment philosophy centers on supporting founders who are building category-defining companies with global impact potential.

Beyond investing, I'm deeply committed to ecosystem building—from organizing Nicaragua's first fintech event with Mastercard to co-founding initiatives like the Founder Mental Health Pledge and Sweater Fund. I believe exceptional founders can emerge from anywhere, and my mission is to ensure they have the capital and support they need to succeed.
